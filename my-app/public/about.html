About us.

The iniative.

Choose Your Space is a 2022 initiative born to provide atmospheric and meteorological data from different cities in Catalonia, as well as vacation houses and rental apartments. It takes data from the European Union's Earth Observation Programme known as Copernicus, which has several satellites called Sentinel orbiting the Earth. Also, it uses data from Airbnb to study the distribution of apartments and its prices.

The target.

The idea is thought as an easy data source for people of all ages who want to check the conditions of the environment they plan to stay for a short, or long, period of time. It is also adressed to companies that want to apply the parameters provided by this space.
